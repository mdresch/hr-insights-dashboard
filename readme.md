
# HR Insights Dashboard

Welcome to the HR Insights Dashboard! This project provides a comprehensive view of various HR metrics and assessments to help HR leaders make informed decisions.

## Tabs

### HR AI Agent Maturity Assessment
Based on the company HR Score framework, this assessment measures the maturity of critical HR activities and identifies priority areas for improvement.

### Top HR Trends for 2025
Key trends shaping the future of HR and requiring strategic attention from HR leaders.

### Succession Planning Dashboard
A comprehensive view of your organization's leadership pipeline, bench strength, and succession readiness.

### Employee Experience Dashboard
A comprehensive view of your organization's employee experience, engagement trends, and key drivers.

### Employee Value Proposition Assessment
A comprehensive analysis of your organization's EVP performance across key categories, with benchmarking against industry standards.
